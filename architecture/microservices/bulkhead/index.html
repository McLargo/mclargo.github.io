Bulkhead Pattern

It has similar purpose as the Circuit Breaker pattern, but it focuses on isolate different services, dividing them into critical and non-critical services.

If one non-critical service fails, it must not affect the critical services (the ones important for your business).

It can be achieved by use separate sources/resources or different clusters.
